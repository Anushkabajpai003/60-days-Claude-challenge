---

## What is Chain-of-Thought Prompting?

Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting is a prompting technique where the AI is instructed to think through a problem in logical steps instead of jumping directly to the answer.

This approach helps generate more accurate, structured, and explainable outputs, especially for complex tasks such as planning, analysis, decision-making, and problem-solving.

2. Identify strengths.
3. Identify skill gaps.
4. Find the fastest path to the goal.
5. Recommend learning priorities.
6. Suggest projects.
7. Recommend networking strategies.
8. Create milestones and action plans.

## Key Learnings

* Chain-of-Thought prompting improves the quality of AI responses.
* Breaking problems into smaller steps helps AI provide better recommendations.
* Structured prompts lead to more personalized and practical outputs.
* AI can act as a strategic planning assistant when guided properly.
* Clear instructions significantly improve response quality.

---

## Biggest Observation

Without structured reasoning, AI often provides generic advice.

When Chain-of-Thought prompting is used, the recommendations become:

* More logical
* More personalized
* More actionable
* Better aligned with the user's goals
