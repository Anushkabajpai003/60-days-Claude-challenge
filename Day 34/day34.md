## Key Learnings

1. Vanity metrics can hide real performance problems.
2. Reach is less important than relevance.
3. Customer feedback often reveals issues before analytics do.
4. Marketing investigations require connecting multiple data points.
5. Strong audience segmentation improves conversion efficiency.
6. Interactive learning makes marketing concepts easier to understand.

---

## Reflection

Building Marketing Detective demonstrated how storytelling and gamification can transform marketing education into an engaging investigative experience. By analyzing clues, reviewing campaign data, and identifying root causes, users learn to think critically about marketing performance rather than relying on surface-level metrics.
