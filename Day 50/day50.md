# Day 50 – Defend Your Experience

## Overview

**Defend Your Experience** is an AI-powered interview preparation application that helps users confidently defend every claim they make about themselves.

Unlike traditional resume review tools, this application acts as an adaptive interviewer. It extracts meaningful claims from uploaded documents and continuously challenges users with personalized follow-up questions, helping them build stronger, evidence-backed interview responses.

The objective is not to improve a resume—it is to ensure users can confidently explain and defend every achievement, project, and experience during real interviews.

---

## Problem

Many candidates write impressive resumes but struggle when interviewers ask:

- Can you explain this project?
- What exactly did you contribute?
- How did you measure success?
- What challenges did you face?
- Why did you choose this approach?

Strong resumes often fail because candidates cannot confidently defend their own experiences.

---

## Solution

Defend Your Experience transforms uploaded documents into an interactive AI interview.

Instead of generic interview questions, the application:

- Extracts claims directly from user documents
- Questions every accomplishment
- Detects vague statements
- Requests measurable evidence
- Builds confidence through continuous practice
- Generates personalized improvement suggestions

Every conversation is unique because it is based entirely on the user's own experience.

# How It Works

1. Upload a document describing your experience.
2. The AI extracts meaningful claims.
3. Every claim becomes an interview topic.
4. The AI challenges each claim with adaptive follow-up questions.
5. Your answers influence future questions.
6. Weak areas are identified.
7. A personalized Defense Report is generated.
