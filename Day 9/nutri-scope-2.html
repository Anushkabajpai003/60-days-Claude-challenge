Nutrient Targets Rationale
Protein — 1.6 g/kg body weight (ISSN position stand for active adults; 1.2 g/kg for sedentary).
Fat — 25% of TDEE (AMDR: 20–35%). Minimum 0.5 g/kg for hormonal health.
Carbohydrates — Remainder of calories after protein and fat allocation.
Fiber — 30 g/day (ICMR 2020 guidelines for Indian adults).
Iron — 8 mg/day (men); 18 mg/day (women of reproductive age) per ICMR RDA.
Calcium — 1000 mg/day for adults (WHO / ICMR).
Vitamin C — 65 mg/day (ICMR); 75–90 mg/day (NIH).
Vitamin D — 15 mcg (600 IU) per day for adults under 70 (IOM/NIH).
Vitamin B12 — 2.4 mcg/day (NIH); higher risk in vegetarians and vegans.
Educational Disclaimer
NutriScope is an educational and informational tool only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Nutrient values in this app are approximate averages and may vary significantly based on cooking method, food variety, soil quality, and portion measurement accuracy.

Do not use NutriScope to manage a medical condition, eating disorder, or any clinical nutritional need. Always seek the advice of a registered dietitian (RD) or licensed physician before making significant dietary changes. If you are pregnant, breastfeeding, under 18, elderly, or managing a chronic condition, consult a healthcare professional before relying on any nutrition calculations.

The BMI scale used in this app is a population-level screening tool and does not account for muscle mass, bone density, ethnicity, or individual health status.
