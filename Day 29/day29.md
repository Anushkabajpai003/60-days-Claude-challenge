# Day 29 — Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## Overview

Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab is an interactive business simulation designed to teach supply chain management, crisis response, negotiation, leadership, and operational resilience through hands-on decision-making.

Players take on the role of a Supply Chain Director responsible for guiding a company through unexpected disruptions while balancing cost, customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and long-term resilience.

---

## Project Objective

The goal of this project was to make complex supply chain concepts accessible to beginners through:

* Scenario-based learning
* Guided decision-making
* Real-world business trade-offs
* Interactive simulations
* Visual performance dashboards

Rather than reading theory, users experience the consequences of their decisions in a realistic business environment.

---

## Core Features

### Company Generator

Randomly generates a fictional company including:

* Industry
* Revenue
* Factories
* Warehouses
* Supplier network
* Inventory days
* Lead times
* Operating countries

### Crisis Generator

Each playthrough includes a different crisis:

* Factory Fire
* Supplier Bankruptcy
* Port Strike
* Cyberattack
* Regional Flood
* Raw Material Shortage
* Political Conflict
* Shipping Delay

Every crisis includes urgency levels, business impact analysis, and beginner-friendly explanations.

### War Room Decision Engine

Players select three strategic response actions from multiple available options.

Performance metrics affected:

* Cost Control
* Inventory Health
* Profitability
* Delivery Speed
* Customer Satisfaction

### Supplier Negotiation Simulator

Four-round branching negotiation experience.

Measures:

* Trust
* Price Advantage
* Lead Time Performance

Players learn how relationship management impacts supply chain stability.

### CEO Boardroom Challenges

Five executive-level scenarios testing:

* Leadership
* Crisis Management
* Strategic Thinking
* Communication
* Decision Making

### AI Strategy Planning

Players choose two AI investments:

* Demand Forecasting AI
* Inventory Optimization Engine
* Supplier Risk Monitor
* Warehouse Vision AI
* Procurement Copilot

Each investment displays expected business impact and resilience improvements.

### Final Executive Dashboard

Comprehensive performance review including:

* Overall Crisis Score
* Leadership Score
* Negotiation Score
* Risk Management
* Cost Control
* Customer Satisfaction
* Personalized Feedback
* Best Decision
* Biggest Mistake
* Expert Recommendations
* Key Lessons Learned

## Key Learnings

* Supply chains are built on resilience, not just efficiency.
* Customer trust is often more valuable than short-term profit.
* Supplier relationships become critical during disruptions.
* Effective crisis communication reduces long-term damage.
* AI can significantly improve forecasting, visibility, and risk management.
* Leadership decisions determine how quickly organizations recover from disruption.

## Reflection

This project challenged me to combine business education, simulation design, and frontend development into a single experience.

The most interesting part was creating a system that teaches users *why* decisions matter instead of simply telling them what to do.

Every playthrough creates a unique learning experience through randomized companies, crises, decisions, and outcomes.
